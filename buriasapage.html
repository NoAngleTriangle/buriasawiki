Buriasan Wiki

 



        
Overview

Buriasa is the second largest Scratch army, below the Republic of Scratch. It is owned by Sable Keystone
 
Buriasa originated as a [Classified] Union Republic created by @Thegiriman2 
on October 19, 2024. Buriasa gained independence from the [Classified] Union on 11/1/2024. 
In June 2025, Buriasa grew an upwards of 100 followers a day before @Thegiriman2 accidentally deleted the studio. Later,
Buriasa conquered The Great Battery Army and turned it into the new Buriasa.


History


On October 19, 2024, @Thegiriman2 (co-owner of the [Classified] Union) asked @mastergamerx-details (owner of the [Classified] Union)  
if he could make "republics" or "branches" of the [Classified] Union. @mastergamerx-details agreed.
There was already a branch called "Welstarr" (formerly Kingdom of Welstarr) and @Thegiriman2 created 2 more republics:
"Ezaria" and "Buriasa". Then on November 1, 2024 (known as "The Triple One" in modern [Classified]) the   
[Classified] Union was overtaken by @Thegiriman2 and given to The Popcorn Uprising.
Then, the "Imperial Rebels of [Classified] (IROC)" was created by @Thegiriman2. Little information is known of this entity.
On March 13, 2025, the Burisan Empire was created.
